How The Scoring Works

Every career recommendation uses this explicit, traceable formula:

score = (skill_match × 45%)
+ (aptitude_match × 35%)
+ (interest_match × 15%)
+ (parent_input × 5%)

Skill gaps are classified as CRITICAL (weight ≥15%, score <50%), IMPORTANT (weight ≥8%, score <70%), or OPTIONAL. Same inputs always produce the same outputs.
